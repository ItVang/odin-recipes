Garlic Fried Chicken

Description:

Traditional fried chicken with a nice and flavorful garlic flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tsp Garlic Powder (or to taste)
  • 1 tsp Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1/2 cup Seasoned Bread Crumbs
  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 4 Skinless, Boneless Chicken Breast Halves (pounded thin)(
  • 1 cup Oil (for frying))

    • Steps:

    1. In a shallow dish, mix together mix together the garlic powder, pepper, salt, paprika, bread cumbs and flour. In a seperate dish, whisk together the milk and egg.

    2. Heat the oil in an electric skill set to 350 degrees F (165 degrees C). Dip the chicken into the egg and milk, then dredge in the dry ingredients until evenly coated.

    3. Fry chicken in the hot oil for about 5 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and juices run clear. Remove from the oil with a slotted spatula, and serve.
